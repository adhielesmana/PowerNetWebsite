PowerNetWebsite presents the PowerNet line of business services through a responsive, single-page layout that prioritizes accessibility and performance. It blends HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript to keep the experience lightweight while showcasing key messaging, call-to-action links, and visual assets.

The build pipeline centers on Nginx for static delivery; configuration is duplicated between a host-based profile (with live TLS generation/support) and a Docker Compose snapshot so teams can deploy consistently in both on-prem and container-driven environments.

Development and operations are coordinated by the PowerNet Systems team. Reach us at ops@powernet.example.com for deployment clarifications, SSL handoff details, or to schedule a presentation about the architecture and design approach.
